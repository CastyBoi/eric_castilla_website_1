Photography and Cars

I like to take pictures, and I like cars.

Theres something about the art of composition, and capturing a moment that does the soul good.

Similarly, I find immense satisfaction when undertaking a repair or upgrade to a vehicle, struggling through unforseen obstacles, and finally acomplishing the task.
Feels nice to do something with your hands once in a while.

Camera Progression

The first camera I used was a Sony A6000. This was a great entry level place to start, and there was a plethora of lenses availabe for various scenarios.

I eventually upgraded to a Sony A7ii, and I LOVED the photos it was able to produce. The Field of View was much greater, and my lightroom skills slowly got better. (I did eventually sell this camera as I stopped using it for over a year.)

After selling the Sony, I was gifted a Panasonic Lumix G100. I still have this one, and it has proven itself to be very easy to carry and use.

More recently I have been using a Canon F5D MKii. Its an oldie but a goldie, and the full frame sensor lets me get back that Field of View I am missing in the Lumix.
